Testing initial
designs

3/5 participants struggled to access goals from multiple areas, pointing to a need for more entry points.

3/5 participants felt lost in the flow, highlighting the need for clearer navigation cues.

3/5 participants found the “create new group” form too long, suggesting it should be more engaging and split into smaller steps.

Methodology Unmoderated usability study
KPIs Time on task, user error rate, system usability scale
Participants Two males and three females, ages 20–50. One had a visual impairment, another a cognitive disability
