Refining groups

To create a more engaging group setup, I broke the flow into multiple pages and added visual cues and feedback.

Testing round 2

Participants wanted detailed breakdowns, even for evenly split expenses, showing a need for more clarity in the pay/request flow.

They also wanted deeper insights and varied visualizations to better understand their data.

Participants continued to struggle finding their goals, pointing to a need for clearer button and icon labels.
Methodology Unmoderated usability study
KPIs Time on task, user error rate, system usability scale
Participants Two males and three females, ages 20–50. One had a visual impairment, another a cognitive disability

Refining round 2: Splitting expenses

To improve clarity around expense splitting, I added custom split options that can be mixed with even splits. The default view now shows a breakdown, even for equal splits.
