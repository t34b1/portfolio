One identity, many forms

Inspired by brands like Apple and Humans of New York, We’re Not Really Strangers is built on simplicity, letting the human experience speak for itself.

Across digital channels, we built a visual language of limited color, generous white space, and clear typography. hotography was raw and unfiltered, like something anyone could capture on a smartphone.

Our brand voice was curious, confrontational, friendly, and flawed—all at once. It avoided fluff or jargon, instead speaking in ways that could be universally understood.
