What looks and sounds true to the brand?



We’re Not Really Strangers is guided by simplicity and honesty. Inspired by brands like Apple and Humans of New York, we let the human experience speak for itself.

We used a limited color palette, and our core visual pillars were generous white space, clear typography, and imagery that evoked familiar feelings. Photography had to feel raw and unfiltered, like something you’d capture on a smartphone.

Our brand voice was friendly, curious, wise, confrontational, and flawed, all at once. It avoided fluff or jargon, speaking only in ways that could be universally understood—and felt.
