For both loyal fans and strangers, social content needed to connect instantly

In a noisy, scrolling environment, engaging with our brand had to feel effortless, and worth it. We aimed for content that was visually simple, instantly understood, and emotionally sharp.

Once subscribed, users expect polished brand emails

Instead, we challenged convention by sending one-word messages, turning links into punchlines, and playing with what a brand email could look like in their inbox.

Our goal was to encourage curiosity, subvert expectations and make every click feel like a discovery.

Challenges

The challenge with email was limited space within cluttered UI, varying screen sizes, and different platform constraints: not ideal for immersive digital experiences.

As a solution, I built templates that stripped away what wasn't necessary for our purpose. My goal was to treat the screen as a personal canvas in the user’s pocket.
